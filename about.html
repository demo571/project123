Resume

A Government Servant by Destiny. Media Manager, Lobbyist and Strategist by profession. Media Faculty teaching Modern Journalism and Public Relations, Best Selling Content Author by choice.

Dr. Sharma is a five-time National Award Winning PR, Content and Media Management professional who has served many VVIP dignitaries in India. He is also a visiting media expert with IGNOU and several other major accredited universities of India.

A successful blogger, Dr. Sharma enjoys authoring about unknown realms of the art and science of fortune telling. His bestsellers are available in several regional and foreign languages.

His favourite quote is: "Nothing is impossible, if you're committed to the goal."

Dr. Sharma is married to Ranjana Sharma and has two daughters: Sukriti, a businessperson married to author Avijit Suryavanshi; and Sanskriti, a Clinical Psychologist, married to IT-Engineer Vishal Vikram Singh.

With over 32 hyperactive and outstandingly successful years as a PR, Media and Content Strategist.
